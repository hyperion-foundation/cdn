# About Hyperion Foundation CDN
Hyperion Foundation CDN is a website to store our old contents. Most of the contents are for **gaming** purposes, 
and some of the contents are not related to gaming.

We made this website is because to make all our old contents can be played or used by everyone in the world.
All contents must be downloaded at first before you use or play it, and all contents in this website are **FREE** (zero-cost).
So, you can enjoy our content that we didn't previously share to the public.

# Website Tech Stack
This website is successfully made by using [Node.js](https://nodejs.org), [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) & [Nextra](https://nextra.site).
